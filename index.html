HELLO FOLKS!

My name is Dali.

I am a Sicilian designer and I'm new here!

I'm a front-end developer student at Edgemony and I'm trying to understand how the whole coding world works!

I have experience in both print and digital design and have developed creative solutions for a variety of media including catalogues, advertising, point of sale, corporate identities, websites, leaflets, brochures and magazines, helping to promote businesses to potential clients through targeted high quality graphic design. Moreover I have well rounded experience in supporting events, advertising, database organization and sales. I am a quick learner and work well within a team environment. I am also extrovert, friendly character who does her best to put people at ease and help fellow co-workers and/or friends.
